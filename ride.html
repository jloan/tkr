Tacoma Kind Rides

RIDE WITH US!

Riding with us is a great way to support Tacoma Rides. There are multiple ways you can hit the streets with us...

Join a social ride.

Tacoma Kind Rides organizes monthly social rides around Tacoma. These rides are a great way to both ride in community and drop-off donations to TKR volunteers.

TKR is also looking to partner with the many outstanding social rides already happening in Tacoma. Do you organize a ride? Would the folks you ride with be interesting in donating food and other itmes? Get in touch.

Volunteer for a Distro Ride.

If you're interested in riding with us to distribute burritos and hygiene kits, we'd love to have you join us.Distro rides happen on the last Saturday of the month and will start at Wright Park.
