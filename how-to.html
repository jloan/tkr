How We Cook

Process (How we do it.)

  • In a large pot, combine and warm 3 - 4 large cans of refried beans and 3 - 4 large can of whole beans. We like pinto. Salt to taste. You might drain one of the cans of whole beans so things don't get too soupy.(Transfer and keep warm in chafing dish.)
  • Saute 12 - 16lbs of frozen O'brien potatoes. Salt and pepper to taste. Winco brand is pretty good.(Transfer and keep warm in chafing dish.)
  • Beat and scramble 150 eggs. Salt and pepper to taste. Transfer and keep warm in chafing dish.)
  • Beat and scramble 150 eggs. Salt and pepper to taste. Transfer and keep warm in chafing dish.)
  • Have shredded cheese handy.
  • While beans, potatoes and eggs are staying cozy in the chafing dishes, warm tortillas on cooktop.
  • Fill warm tortillas with combination of egg, beans, potatoes, and cheese.
  • Roll burrito.
  • In batches, toast burritos on cooktop.
  • Wrap burritos in foil. Then put them into a cooler or insulated grocery sack to keep warm.
  • Load burritos into bike bags.
  • Ride.

Tools

  • We use a Blackstone Griddle. We it set it in a driveway. Sort of like tailgaiting.
  • Various food preop things: spatula's, serving spoons, food handling gloves, big sanitaized bucket for beating eggs, folding tables, etc...
