DONATE!

Donations are a wonderful way to support Tacoma Kind Rides!

Food items we need:

  • Flour tortillas -- burrito size!
  • Beans -- refried and whole; pinto or black. (We love big cans!)
  • Eggs.
  • Potatoes -- frozen. (O'brien potatoes are a breakfast burritos best friend!)
  • Cheese -- shredded.
  • Vegatable oil.

To donate food items reach out to us on IG @tacomakindrides so we can coordinate dropoff or pick-up of perishable items.

Non-perishables we need on the regular:

  • Foil.
  • Sternos.
  • Food handling gloves.
  • Paper towels.

To donate non-persihable drop them off with our partners at Opalescent Cyclery so we can coordinate dropoff or pick-up of perishable items.

Some food prep tools we use/need:

  • Propane!
  • Chafing dishes.
  • Warming drawers.

If you're interested in donating any of these hefty-er essentails, again -- touchbase with us on IG @tacomakindrides so we can work with you on this.
